Our Commitment

Made When Ordered

We dehydrate treats in small batches using time honored techniques instead of high heat thermal extrusion or other weird stuff. It’s not complicated, it’s just the right thing to do.

Made In USA

We craft all treats and chews in our own home, instead of outsourcing the production to contract manufacturers. This has given us the unique control over the quality we wanted to have. Proudly Made in the USA.

Human Grade

We start with 100% Human-Grade ingredients. In other words, real chicken breast, sweet potatoes, blueberries. Things you find in a grocery store and likely eat yourself. Free of any additives, chemicals, fillers or preservatives.

About Us

Hello, We are Christine and Jason Baird, owners of Savory Thyme Charcuterie, LLC. We are a Rio Rancho, New Mexico Based food truck. Savory Thyme was inspired by our love for quality meats and decadent cheeses. It all started with making charcuterie boards for friends and family. A few cheese boards later we felt the food truck scene had a missing link and so Savory Thyme was born. We are excited to see where this journey takes us and we look forward to building a bite just for you! We hope you'll experience the same happiness we enjoy while tasting one of our creations. See you out there!
