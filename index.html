Our Commitment

Made When Ordered

We dehydrate treats in small batches using time honored techniques instead of high heat thermal extrusion or other weird stuff. It’s not complicated, it’s just the right thing to do.

Made In USA

We craft all treats and chews in our own home, instead of outsourcing the production to contract manufacturers. This has given us the unique control over the quality we wanted to have. Proudly Made in the USA.

Human Grade

We start with 100% Human-Grade ingredients. In other words, real chicken breast, sweet potatoes, blueberries. Things you find in a grocery store and likely eat yourself. Free of any additives, chemicals, fillers or preservatives.

About Us

In recent history I became interested in the art of charcuterie and began creating boards and boxes for special occasions, friends, family or simply for lunch. I have also always been interested and involved in feeding my 2 dogs the best food & treats. I wanted to combine my love of charcuterie with my love of animals, however, perishable ingredients would not be an option, I wanted to appeal to ALL the pets in the U.S. so I dreamt up the Pawcuterie Box! A box for pets containing shelf stable healthy snacks for your furfriend(s). I bought a dehydrator & got to work. I'm excited to introduce a whole new world of treats to fellow dog and cat parents! To provide these all-natural goodies, I use low to single-ingredients and create in small batches. ENJOY!
